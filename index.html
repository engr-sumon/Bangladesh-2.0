Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh innovation drives digital growth, empowering youth, startups, technology, sustainability, and global competitiveness.

Sustainability

Sustainability ensures responsible growth, protecting environment, resources, communities, economy, and future generations worldwide.

Infrastructure

Strong infrastructure supports economic growth, connectivity, resilience, innovation, services, productivity, and national development.

Empowerment

Empowerment builds confidence skills opportunities equality leadership independence and sustainable progress for communities.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Digital transformation modernizes systems, boosts efficiency, and enhances services through technology.

Green Energy

Green energy powers homes, reduces pollution, supports sustainability, and combats climate change.

Modern Infrastructure

Modern infrastructure strengthens connectivity, supports growth, improves services, and boosts national development.

"The vision of the future is a world where technology and humanity work together harmoniously, creating smarter cities, greener energy systems, and stronger communities. Innovation will drive education, healthcare, and businesses to become more accessible and efficient, while sustainable practices protect our environment and resources. People will be empowered through equal opportunities, digital skills, and inclusive growth. Collaboration across nations will solve global challenges, ensuring peace, prosperity, and a better quality of life for every generation, shaping a brighter and more hopeful tomorrow."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
